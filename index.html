Life of Mas

Hello my name is Mason Moore. I am from Chelsea Alabama. I graduated form Chelsea High School. I have a lot of hobbies but my main ones are playing video games, long boarding, playing any sport, hiking, rock climbing, and dancing. I played high school football, baseball, and basketball. I loved all three of these sports but I had to chose one to stick with my Junior and Senior year so I chose football. When I was in high school I really had no idea where I wanted to go to College. Highlands College was actually not even on the radar. I was looking more towards Jeff State and then transferring to Alabama. When I was starting to apply for college's I had a friend who goes to Highlands College tell me to check it out. When I looked into it, I felt God calling me to Highlands College. It just felt like home.
